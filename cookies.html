Cookie Policy

Yes, we love cookies — but these are the digital kind. Here's how we use them on our website.

🍪 TL;DR

We use cookies to make your experience sweeter — remembering your cart, preferences, and helping us improve. You can manage your preferences anytime.

🍪 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your actions and preferences (like login details, language, and display preferences) over a period of time, so you don't have to re-enter them whenever you come back.

Despite sharing a name with our delicious baked goods, these cookies are purely digital — no sprinkles involved.

📋 Types of Cookies We Use

🔒 Essential Cookies

These are required for the website to function properly. They enable core features like:

  • Shopping cart functionality
  • Secure checkout process
  • Account authentication
  • Security and fraud prevention

Can you disable these? No — they're necessary for the site to work.

📊 Analytics Cookies

These help us understand how visitors use our website so we can improve it:

  • Which pages are most popular
  • How visitors navigate through the site
  • Where errors occur
  • Time spent on pages

All analytics data is anonymized. We use Google Analytics with IP anonymization enabled.

🎯 Functional Cookies

These remember your choices and provide enhanced, personalized features:

  • Your flavor preferences and saved favorites
  • Recently viewed products
  • Language and region settings
  • Display preferences (dark mode, etc.)

📣 Marketing Cookies

These are used to deliver relevant advertisements and track campaign effectiveness:

  • Showing you donuts you've browsed on other sites
  • Measuring ad click-through rates
  • Limiting how often you see the same ad

These are only set with your consent and can be disabled at any time.

📝 Cookie Details

Cookie Name Type Duration Purpose
doughy_session Essential Session Maintains your shopping session
doughy_cart Essential 30 days Remembers cart contents
doughy_auth Essential 7 days Keeps you logged in
doughy_prefs Functional 1 year Stores your display preferences
_ga / _gid Analytics 2 years / 24h Google Analytics tracking
doughy_consent Essential 1 year Remembers your cookie preferences

⚙️ Managing Your Cookies

You have full control over cookies. Here's how to manage them:

  • Browser settings: Most browsers let you block or delete cookies via settings. Check your browser's help section for instructions.
  • Our cookie banner: When you first visit, you can accept all cookies or choose which categories to enable.
  • Opt out of analytics: Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Note: Disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality (e.g., your cart might not save between visits).

🔄 Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the date below and notify you via a banner if the changes are significant.
