📍 Delivery Zones & Fees

We deliver based on distance from our bakery. Here's the breakdown:

Zone Distance Est. Time Fee
🟢 Core Zone 0 – 5 miles 30 min FREE
🟡 Extended 5 – 10 miles 45 min $2.99
🟠 Outer 10 – 15 miles 60 min $4.99
🔴 Special 15+ miles Contact us Custom

💡 Pro Tip

Orders over $25 always ship free within Core and Extended zones! Add one more donut to hit the threshold — you won't regret it.

🕐 Delivery Hours

We deliver during the following hours:

  • Monday – Friday: 7:00 AM – 9:00 PM
  • Saturday: 8:00 AM – 10:00 PM
  • Sunday: 8:00 AM – 8:00 PM
  • Holidays: Limited hours (check our socials for updates)

Orders placed outside these hours will be scheduled for the next available delivery window.

📦 Our Packaging Promise

Every order is packed with care to ensure your donuts arrive in perfect condition:

  • Insulated boxes to keep donuts warm for up to 30 minutes
  • Eco-friendly materials — 100% recyclable packaging
  • Tamper-proof seals so you know your donuts are safe
  • Individual slots to prevent squishing or sliding
