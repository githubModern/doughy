🔒 🛡️ Your Data, Protected

Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously — almost as seriously as we take our donut recipes.

🔒 Our Promise

We collect only what we need, we never sell your data, and we protect it like we protect our secret glaze recipe. Your trust matters more than anything.

📋 Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you create an account, place an order, or contact us, we may collect:

  • Personal details: Name, email address, phone number
  • Delivery info: Shipping address, delivery instructions
  • Payment info: Card details (processed securely via Stripe — we never store your full card number)
  • Account data: Username, password (encrypted), order history
  • Communications: Messages you send us, reviews, feedback

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • Device data: Browser type, operating system, screen size
  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent, clicks
  • Location data: General location (city-level, for delivery zone estimation)
  • Cookies: See our Cookie Policy for full details

🎯 How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process orders — bake, pack, and deliver your donuts
  • Manage your account — loyalty points, order history, preferences
  • Improve our service — analyze trends, fix bugs, develop new flavors
  • Communicate with you — order updates, promotions (only if you opt in), support
  • Prevent fraud — protect against unauthorized transactions
  • Legal compliance — meet tax, accounting, and regulatory requirements

🤝 Data Sharing

We never sell your personal data. Period. We only share data with:

  • Payment processors (Stripe) — to process transactions securely
  • Delivery partners — name and address to deliver your order
  • Analytics tools (Google Analytics) — anonymized usage data only
  • Email service (if opted in) — to send order updates and newsletters
  • Law enforcement — only when legally required
🔐

Encrypted

All data is encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest (AES-256).

🚫

Never Sold

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

🗑️

Right to Delete

Request deletion of your data anytime. We'll comply within 30 days.

⚙️ Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correct — update or fix inaccurate information
  • Delete — request deletion of your personal data
  • Port — receive your data in a portable format
  • Object — opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Restrict — limit how we process your data in certain situations

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@doughy.com. We'll respond within 30 days.

👶 Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or a banner on our website. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
