Refund Policy

Your happiness is our priority. If something went wrong, we'll make it right.

💖 Our Guarantee

If you're not 100% happy with your order, contact us within 24 hours. We'll fix it — no questions, no hassle, no donut left behind.

🔄 How to Request a Refund

Getting a refund is easy — just follow these simple steps:

📸

Document the Issue

Take a photo of the problem — damaged donuts, wrong items, or anything off.

📧

Contact Us

Email us at support@doughy.com with your order number and photo within 24 hours.

🔍

We Review

Our team will review your request within 1 business day and get back to you.

✅

Get Resolved

We'll offer a refund, replacement, or store credit — your choice!

✅ Eligible for Refund

You're eligible for a full refund or replacement if:

  • Wrong items delivered — you received donuts you didn't order
  • Damaged in transit — squished, crushed, or visibly damaged
  • Missing items — your order is incomplete
  • Quality issues — stale, undercooked, or not up to our standards
  • Late delivery — order arrived more than 1 hour past the estimated window
  • Allergic reaction concern — wrong allergen info listed on the product page

❌ Not Eligible for Refund

We can't offer refunds for:

  • Change of mind — "I wanted chocolate, not strawberry" after delivery
  • Incorrect address — if you provided the wrong delivery address
  • No one home — if nobody was available to receive the delivery
  • After 24 hours — refund requests submitted more than 24 hours after delivery
  • Consumed products — if the entire order has been eaten (we get it, but still)

💳 Refund Options

💰

Full Refund
